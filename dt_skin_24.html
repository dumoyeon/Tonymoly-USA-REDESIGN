WHAT MAKES IT GOOD!

This powerful toner fights wrinkles and free radicals that may accelerate signs of aging, brightening, and firming the skin without being aggressive. It replenishes dull, dry skin with rich, hydrating nutrients making skin look youthful and radiant and feel soft to the touch.

Our Black Tea collection features Britain’s Premium Tea Company, Taylors of Harrogate. Taylors, founded in 1886 handles only the highest quality tea leaves and has won many awards for their high-quality teas. We combined 100% pure Taylors black tea with special probiotics to create our own unique patented fermented Black Tea extract, which is packed with antioxidants and has potent anti-aging properties.
